sidebarTitle: On-ramp
---


Since the Embedded Wallet product can be integrated with [Web SDK](/sdk/web/overview), a developer can allow their users to purchase fiat via a credit or debit card with the use of a simplified react component.

Integration steps can be found [here](/sdk/web/guides/on-ramp)
