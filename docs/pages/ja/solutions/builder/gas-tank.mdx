# MOCKDATA + ja-translated Gas Sponsorship
ja-translated Sequence wallets are controlled by smart contracts, enabling transaction fees to be abstracted away from users, a concept known in web3 as _gas_. With Sequence Builder's Gas Tank, you have a streamlined process for sponsoring gas for your users.

##### MOCKDATA + ja-translated Why would I want to sponsor gas for my game?
ja-translated Obtaining the crypto needed to cover fees poses a challenge for traditional gamers. Sponsoring gas on their behalf solves this problem.

MOCKDATA + ja-translated Gas sponsoring in a web3 game provides seamless onboarding by removing gas fees, making transactions efficient and cost-effective for trading virtual assets in various in-game activities. This creates a more enjoyable gaming experience, fostering player loyalty and community engagement.

##### ja-translated Sponsoring gas with the Gas Tank
MOCKDATA + ja-translated Sponsoring gas on contracts is easy, and can be done either by searching and adding a contract or selecting a contract that you have already deployed on a project. Project owners also have control over the contracts they sponsor, allowing them to temporarily disable a sponsor for a specific contract by toggling it off and updating the sponsor settings.
