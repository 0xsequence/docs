# ja-translated Launch your White-label Marketplace
MOCKDATA + ja-translated Sequence Builder provides game builders with a white-label marketplace that can be launched in seconds. With Builder, you can customize your marketplace and integrate it directly into your game experience, all with no-coding experience necessary. Watch a [video to get started](/solutions/marketplaces/white-label-marketplace/video-walkthrough#watch-a-marketplace-be-launched-in-minutes), or, follow [several steps to launch a marketplace](/solutions/marketplaces/white-label-marketplace/guide).

## ja-translated Try it out
MOCKDATA + ja-translated :::info
Get started quickly at [Sequence Builder](https://sequence.build/) to launch your marketplace.
:::

##### ja-translated Why would I want my own Marketplace for my web3 game?
MOCKDATA + ja-translated Builder gives you full control over everything in your marketplace, from enforcing royalties (with ERC-2981) to
choosing the best trading mechanism for your community, setting fees, and more.
You can also aggregate listings from major platforms like OpenSea or LooksRare, providing you with the benefit of existing liquidity without relinquishing control of the gaming experience.

## MOCKDATA + ja-translated Read several steps to Launch a Marketplace

### ja-translated Create Project
MOCKDATA + ja-translated Complete [steps 1-5 of the following guide to create a project](/solutions/builder/getting-started#get-started).

### ja-translated Optional: Create Collection
MOCKDATA + ja-translated If you already have a collection, skip to the next step, or, complete [the following guide to create a collectible](/solutions/collectibles/contracts/deploy-an-item-collection) and [add a collection to your collectible](/solutions/builder/collections) to include on your marketplace.

### ja-translated Deploy White-label Marketplace
MOCKDATA + ja-translated After accessing your project, and the `Marketplace` nav tab in the `Monetize` dropdown, select `Create my marketplace`

MOCKDATA + ja-translated ![deploy new marketplace](/img/marketplace/builder_deploy_new_marketplace.png)

### ja-translated Add a Collection to Marketplace
MOCKDATA + ja-translated Next, select the `+ Add contract` button, and copy and paste a contract address into the field to search and include on the marketplace.

ja-translated ![add new contract to marketplace](/img/marketplace/builder_add_new_contract_to_marketplace.png)

MOCKDATA + ja-translated Repeat this step until you are happy with your marketplace curation.

### ja-translated Choose Marketplace Type
MOCKDATA + ja-translated For each Collectible, choose the type of marketplace you want your users to transact in.

ja-translated ![choose marketplace type](/img/marketplace/builder_markeplace_setting_update_marketplace_type.png)

#### MOCKDATA + ja-translated Sequence Market
ja-translated This marketplace type enables users to create custom listings and offers on a decentralized orderbook, available for anyone to partake in commerce, allowing for time expiry of orders, custom pricing, and ERC20 custom currency types, as well as other features.

#### MOCKDATA + ja-translated P2P
ja-translated This marketplace type sources from various marketplaces on the internet, like Opensea, Blur, Magic Eden, LooksRare, SudoSwap, NFTX, etc. For this marketplace type, there is no custom currency.

#### MOCKDATA + ja-translated AMM
ja-translated This marketplace type enables users to trade ERC1155 tokens using the Niftyswap protocol, which creates liquidity pools with any ERC20 token through an Automated Market Maker (AMM) mechanism.

##### MOCKDATA + ja-translated `Custom Subdomain`
ja-translated Add a custom domain so that users can access the marketplace from a public URL of your choosing that is not randomly generated.

##### MOCKDATA + ja-translated `Select Sequence Wallet Type`

##### MOCKDATA + ja-translated `Enable EIP-6963 Wallets`
ja-translated EIP-6963, titled "Multi Injected Provider Discovery," is an Ethereum Improvement Proposal that introduces a standardized method for decentralized applications (dApps) to detect and interact with multiple wallet providers injected into a web page so that wallet don't overlap in the inject javascript global object.
