# How to Deploy an Item Collection Contract in Sequence Builder

This guide walks through how to setup and deploy the contracts for launching a primary sale - suitable for an NFT Drop, In-game Store, and more.

<Callout type="warning">
Prerequisite: Create a Project
This guide assumes that you have already [signed up for Builder and created a Project](/solutions/builder/getting-started).
</Callout>

## Step 1: Navigate to contracts

Start by selecting your `project` in the top left and corner for what you want to create the collectible for, and head to the `contracts` section and select 

![select project and new contract](/img/collectibles/deploy_new_contract.png)

## Step 2: Deploy your chosen Collectible contract

Follow the [guide](/solutions/collectibles/contracts/deploy-an-item-collection) in order to deploy a collectible contract as well as upload the collection metadata that you wish to create a sale for.

![select game item](/img/collectibles/select_web3_game_item.png)

## Step 3: Deploy your Primary Sales contract

Once successfully deployed, return to the contracts page and select `+ Deploy new contract` again, selecting the appropriate sale that corresopnds (ERC721 or ERC1155) contract for the previous collectible contract you deployed in step 2.

![Deploy Sale Contract](/img/collectibles/primary-sales/docs_deploy_sale.png)


## Step 4: Set your mint details on the Primary Sales contract.

Navigate to the mint details tab for your primary sales contract. Fill out your corresponding mint details with your desired sale parameters

![Mint Access](/img/collectibles/primary-sales/docs_mint_access.png)

<Callout type="tip">
If the current time is within the date range provided, this will activate the sale.
Consider setting the date range to the future or performing this step after completing the rest of the configuration.
</Callout>


## Step 5: Add MinterRole for Sales Contract to Collection Contract

Navigate to the previously deployed Items contract, select the Write Contract tab, and call the `grantRole` function to grant the Sale contract the `MINTER_ROLE`.

You will want to pass in:

- `0x9f2df0fed2c77648de5860a4cc508cd0818c85b8b8a1ab4ceeef8d981c8956a6` for the role field, which is the keccak256 hash of the string `MINTER_ROLE`.
- Address of the Sale Contract you previously deployed.

Then click 
![Grant Minter Role](/img/collectibles/primary-sales/docs_minter_role.png)
