# Overview

Sequence Shop offers a solution to facilitate Primary Sales of your NFTs to your users.

##### Why would I want a Shop for my web3 game?

Builder gives you comprehensive control over every aspect of your shop. You can aggregate listings, manage stock, set deadlines for your listings, and more.
